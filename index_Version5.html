Aggregator vs direct

Aggregator or direct integrations?

Compare the impact on speed, cost, and team workload.

Aggregation advantages

Launch new studios in days, keep a single technical contract, and rely on one set of compliance and reporting tools. Operations teams gain a unified view of uptime, performance, and payments without hopping between portals.

Direct integration trade-offs

Requires multiple SDKs, separate legal terms, individual release cycles, and additional monitoring coverage. This model can be effective for a small catalog but scales slowly.
